ABOUT

Sanskritik Chetna Parishad is a University Level Professional Body, owned and guided by Dean Student Welfare, GBPUAT, Pantnagar. It is a decades-old brilliant organization which has been working for skill-development of students at the university level, for organizing University and national level conferences and debates, and for promotion of our uniqueness in cultural, national and ethical values among all.
